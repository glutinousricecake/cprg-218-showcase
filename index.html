Most of us enjoy watching movies to chill, relax, and escape into different worlds. Whether it's the heartwarming stories from Disney, the imaginative tales from Pixar, or the action-packed adventures from Marvel, many of us have a soft spot for these films, no matter our age. These movies have a unique way of bringing people together, sparking joy, and leaving a lasting impact on our lives.

For the list below, here are six of the most beloved movies I've enjoyed throughout the years. Each one holds a special place in my heart, and I hope they bring you as much joy as they've brought me.

Finding Nemo (2003)

Finding Nemo is one of Pixar’s most beloved animated films, known for its breathtaking underwater visuals and heartfelt storytelling. It follows Marlin, a clownfish who is overly protective of his only son, Nemo, after a tragic loss. When Nemo gets captured by a diver and placed in a fish tank, Marlin embarks on an epic journey across the ocean to rescue him. Along the way, he meets unforgettable characters like the forgetful but lovable Dory and the laid-back sea turtle Crush. The film is fun, adventurous, and never has a dull moment, making it a timeless classic. It’s a childhood favorite for many, and its humour and emotional depth make it one of the best Pixar movies of all time. The story also teaches valuable lessons about trust, perseverance, and letting go.

Soul (2020)

A beautifully animated and thought-provoking film, Soul explores life, purpose, and passion through a unique perspective. It follows Joe Gardner, a middle school band teacher and passionate jazz musician who dreams of making it big. However, just as he gets his big break, an accident sends him to the Great Before—a realm where souls develop their personalities before being born. With stunning visuals and a heartwarming message, the movie makes you reflect on what truly matters in life beyond ambition. One of the most memorable quotes from the film is: “Is all this living really worth dying for?”—a powerful reminder to appreciate the small joys of life. The film beautifully conveys that purpose isn’t just about achieving a dream but also about finding meaning in everyday experiences.

Zootopia (2016)

Zootopia is a fun and highly entertaining movie set in a world where animals live like humans, each species occupying different social roles. It follows Judy Hopps, a determined and optimistic bunny who dreams of being the first rabbit police officer in the city of Zootopia. Despite facing skepticism and discrimination, she teams up with a sly fox, Nick Wilde, to solve a mysterious case that threatens the city. With clever storytelling, humour, and a strong message about breaking stereotypes, this film is endlessly rewatchable. No matter how many times you see it, it remains just as enjoyable. The movie also does an excellent job of tackling real-world issues like prejudice, bias, and the importance of understanding others beyond first impressions.

Up (2009)

A mix of adventure and heartfelt emotion, Up is an unforgettable Pixar film that balances humour, action, and deep storytelling. The opening sequence, which tells the love story of Carl and Ellie through a silent montage, is one of the most emotional moments in animation history, bringing audiences to tears in just a few minutes. After Ellie’s passing, Carl, now a grumpy old man, sets out on a journey to fulfill their lifelong dream of visiting Paradise Falls by attaching thousands of balloons to his house. Along the way, he unexpectedly gains a travel companion, Russell, a young Wilderness Explorer eager to help. The rest of the film is filled with humour, action, and a heartwarming bond between Carl and Russell as they learn valuable lessons from each other. Up is a perfect blend of fun and deep storytelling, reminding viewers that adventure can be found in the most unexpected places.

Big Hero 6 (2014)

Big Hero 6 is an action-packed, heartfelt film about a young robotics prodigy, Hiro Hamada, and his lovable healthcare companion-turned-superhero, Baymax. The movie explores themes of grief, friendship, and heroism, showing Hiro’s journey of coping with loss and finding purpose again. With the help of Baymax and a group of brilliant friends, he forms a team of high-tech heroes to stop a mysterious villain. The film delivers thrilling action sequences, stunning animation, and touching emotional moments, making it a standout among animated superhero movies. Big Hero 6 perfectly blends humor and heart, with Baymax’s adorable and caring nature making him one of the most memorable characters in modern animation. The movie also emphasizes the power of teamwork and the importance of using intelligence for good.

Avengers: Infinity War (2018)

Few movies have left as big of an impact as Avengers: Infinity War. Bringing together nearly every Marvel superhero from across the franchise, this film delivers intense action, shocking twists, and deeply emotional moments. It follows the villain Thanos as he seeks to collect all six Infinity Stones, believing that wiping out half of all life is the only way to restore balance to the universe. The film masterfully balances multiple storylines, keeping audiences engaged while building up to one of the most devastating endings in blockbuster history. The suspenseful ending left audiences in shock, making it one of the most memorable theater experiences. If I could relive watching a movie for the first time in theaters, this would be the one. The film’s bold storytelling and unexpected outcome cemented its place as one of the greatest superhero movies ever made.
